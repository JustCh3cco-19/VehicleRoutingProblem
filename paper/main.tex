\begin{abstract}
This paper presents the design and analysis of a solver for the Constrained Vehicle Routing Problem based on Ant Colony Optimization. The report is organized around the mathematical formulation of the problem, the structure of the sequential baseline, and the analysis of the parallel implementations developed in the project. Particular attention is devoted to the algorithmic components that dominate execution time, such as probabilistic route construction, solution evaluation, and pheromone management.
\end{abstract}
